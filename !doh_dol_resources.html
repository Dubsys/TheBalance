General FAQ: 1. What specialists should i chose?
It doesn't really matter for now. All specialization does right now is give an additional 20/20/15 to your stats, give access to Careful Observation (which is very much a niche skill right now). It unlocks crafts from the latest Primal EX fight (Chocobo Barding and furnitures) and the latest Glowing Primal Weapons. The crafters change from time to time, so make sure to check which one crafts what if youâ€™re interested. You can change your specialization 3 times a week; each swap costs 480 scrips.
2. Why do I not have Manipulation/Unearth III etc.
Manipulation and several gathering skills/traits are still tied to the class quests between 50 and 70 (and yes, you have to do the quests on each of the crafters/gatherers individually to be able to use the skills on that respective class). It is not advised to skip them as all the skills are used frequently. For example, trying to make a rotation that does not use Manipulation ends up costing significantly more cp in comparison.
3. How does Gatherers Boon work?
Based on your perception and the item you're gathering, you will gain up to a 60% chance to gain +1 yield. Currently, it is not something to fully focus on stat wise.

4. Where are the new class quests from Endwalker? What are they called? How do I do them?
Theyâ€™re called the studium and are available in old sharlayan after completing some of the MSQ. These deliveries are independent from Custom Deliveries and are only 1 time deliveries. There's 5 NPCs, each offering a quest path. All 11 classes interacts with 1 of these quest paths, grouped: [CPR/LTW/WVR], [BSM/ARM/GSM], [ALC/CUL], [MIN/BOT] and [FSH].
5. What are the retainer breakpoints for maximum yield?
We currently only know MIN/BTN, you can check those breakpoints in the gathering calculator. For Ilv, get as high an ilv as you can without using timed currencies.
6. What's great to sell on the Market board?
We can't tell you. Every single server and data center is different, best course of action is to scour your market board and find a niche market yourself. Some people might be able to give some pointers, just be aware that anything said in these channels might lead to a fast market crash.

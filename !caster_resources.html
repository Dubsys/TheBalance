Gearing Due to the large difference in substat preferences between casters, it is not possible to share a single gearset between all three. Black Mage and Red Mage in particular are often at odds with each other due to Spell Speed preferences and will usually not have full gearset or meld overlap. Summoner, on the other hand, can opt for both higher Spell Speed builds similar to Black Mage or lower Spell Speed builds similar to Red Mage. For more information on what gear to use for each caster, check the job-specific resource channels.

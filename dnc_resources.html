Best-in-slot for Old Ultimates: UCoB, UWU, and TEA
With ilvl sync, you can max out on both secondary stats on a gear piece as long as that gear piece is above a certain ilvl based on the encounter's ilvl sync. Recommended Gearsets for these fights are items that are at the ilvl needed to max both secondaries and have Crit and either Det or DH. ilvl for max stats: UCoB: 470 UWU: 500 TEA: 600 The best weapon for all of these ilvl synced fights is the ilvl 535 Save the Queen relic weapon "Blade's Euphoria" with specific stat allocation that maximizes how many secondary stats you can get for the fight: UCoB: 127 Crit, 127 Det, 124 DH, 90 SkS (3 points from DH moved to SkS to match with a 2.46 GCD tier) UWU: 136 Crit, 136 Det, 133 DH, 63 SkS (3 points from DH moved to SkS to match with a 2.47 GCD tier) TEA: 184 Crit, 184 Det, 100 DH, 0 SkS BiS Food: Use current BiS Food BiS Potion: Use current BiS Potion

Samurai Openers
「Standard | Single Weave」 ➜ Your standard opener which will be used in the majority of encounters. Due to phasing, in some instances it may be best to begin with Kasha into Gekko instead. ➜ Most buff windows are between Midare and Hakaze, feel free to shift oGCDs to use double-weaves instead of Iai-weaves if they are easier on your ping. ➜ If you get an early Third Eye proc, move the final Shinten forward and use Gyoten in its place.

Why is Looping Important For Our Rotation?
Despite gaining significant flexibility in our previously rigid abilities Meikyo Shisui and Tsubame-gaeshi, it is still important in a raid scenario to maintain alignment with our party's raid buffs at 60/120s intervals. Because of this, and our desire to keep as much uptime as possible of our 60 second DoT Higanbana, we are still recommending specific GCD ranges to prevent unintentionally clipping our skills early or drifting them outside of these raid buff windows. Each minute past the first contains a 28/29/30-GCD loop, depending on your GCD. Our base rotation and burst totals 26 GCDs. In the opener and every even minute(2/4/6/etc) we use Ogi Namikiri and Kaeshi: Namikiri, which fill two additional spots to bring us to 28. Every odd minute(1/3/5/etc), these skills are on cooldown. → Because of this, we will alternate how many GCDs of filler we need to use for the rotation. Odd minutes where Ogi Namikiri is on cooldown will require 2/3/4, while even minutes where we have it will require 0/1/2. → Refer to the Bushido guide for an in-depth explanation. 「Visual Guide」 「Filler Guide」 Viable GCD/SkS ranges (with Shifu counted) for looping: ____________________________________________________ | Recast with Shifu | SkS Range | Filler Req. | |___________________|_______________|_______________| |2.15 / 2.14 | 503 - 648 | 2 Filler GCDs | |2.08 / 2.07 / 2.06 | 985 - 1,174 | 3 Filler GCDs | |2.01 / 2.00 / 1.99 | 1,453 - 1,584 | 4 Filler GCDs | |___________________|_______________|_______________| → Pros and Cons can be seen in the BIS section below. Filler Options: 1) Yaten-Enpi = 1 Filler GCD 2) Yukikaze-Hagakure = 2 Filler GCDs 3) Gekko-/Kasha-Hagakure = 3 Filler GCDs → Avoid using Yaten-Enpi as filler unless necessary for boss mechanics or when only a single filler GCD is needed.

Highest DPS, easiest weaving. For 2.14 swap 2 DH melds for 2 SKS melds. 2.08 Savage BiS 「Image | Etro」 ➜ 29 GCD loop, 3 filler GCDs ➜ 10,755.76 DPS including 5% party buff. ➜ Small hit in DPS in exchange for a slightly extended rotation, offering more flexibility with mechanic downtime. 2.00 Savage BiS 「Image | Etro」 ➜ 30 GCD loop, 4 filler GCDs ➜ 10,684.28 DPS including 5% party buff. ➜ Classic "Fast SAM".

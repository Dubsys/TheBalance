Opener FAQ Is it really okay to use Radiant Finale for only 2% in the opener?
Yes, by the next two-minute burst you will have gotten three more coda.
Should I use Refulgent Arrow between Stormbite and Caustic Bite if I get a proc?
No, getting both DoTs up as soon as possible is slightly better.
I’ve heard about using Tinctures at two minutes, why is it here?
This is just a default opener if you don’t know the fight length. If you’re certain the fight will last 8:30-9:15 or so (accounting for downtime), you will want to Tincture at two and eight minutes. The opener burst lacks Apex/Blast Arrow and only has a 2% Radiant Finale, so it's best to have Tinctures on full-powered bursts when it doesn't cost a usage.

Single target FAQ How do we get Repertoire?
While you have a song active, you have an 80% chance to get a Repertoire when the song timer hits 42, 39, 36, etc. down to 3 seconds. Note that this makes the last 3 seconds of each song 'empty', so you're free to leave at any point the timer reads 3 or less.
What order do we use our songs in?
Try to do ~43s of WM, ~34s of MB, and ~43s of AP. In practice, this means leaving WM at 3s or less on the timer (use any Pitch Perfect stacks first!). Then leave MB at 12s left or less, and leave AP once WM is available again.
Why do we use Mage's Ballad second if it's the weakest song?
1. We want the Army's Muse buff to land on our 2-minute burst to get an extra GCD under (some) buffs. 2. MB has the stronger party buff to have running when players use their 60-second damage cooldowns. Don't forget that Mage's Ballad is only very slightly weaker than Army's. It's plenty worth taking these gains.
What's the best time to use Apex Arrow?
Ideally, you want to treat it as a pseudo-60s cooldown so that every other one lands in buffs. At around 21-19s left in Mage's Ballad, fire it off so long as you have 80 gauge or more. If you get 100 gauge earlier than that, you can Apex then too. Then, hold on to it until all of your 2-minute buffs are up in Wanderer's Minuet. Even if it hits 100 well in advance of this, just sit on it. It's not worth throwing the timing off.
How do I handle the two-minute burst window?
Start by late-weaving Wanderer's Minuet, this is to make sure you can get five GCDs under Army's Muse. If you've kept Empyreal Arrow on a tight cooldown, you can RS+EA in the next window to keep the EA cooldown rolling. Otherwise, late-weave Raging Strikes and use Empyreal when able. You could be close to capped on Bloodletter there as well; if so use it. Then in the third window, use Battle Voice + Radiant Finale. The three buffs should all be ending around the same time this way. During this stack of buffs, you want to use: Apex Arrow, Blast Arrow, Barrage + Refulgent Arrow, and Iron Jaws. For oGCDs: Sidewinder, an Empyreal Arrow, any Pitch Perfect 3s you get, and ideally three Bloodletters after saving a couple during Army's Paeon. Try also to burn any remaining Pitch Perfect stacks before buffs expire. An example sequence might be: https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/580961239581327360/942275135581331466/BRD_BURST_2.png
Do I delay Empyreal Arrow if I have two Pitch Perfect stacks and a tick coming up?
No, use a 2-stack Pitch Perfect instead. Ideally, use it as soon as you get that tick before the EA, so that you can press EA without delay.
Should I use Iron Jaws under buffs?
Yes. When you use Iron Jaws, the full run of DoT ticks gains all buffs and debuffs that were active at the time of IJ's use. This makes it well worth using it early to catch the 2-minute window.
When do I use Tinctures?
Like many jobs, if the fight lasts long enough to use a Tincture at 2 minutes and 8 minutes, do that. Otherwise, use one pre-pull and the next at 6 minutes.
AoE FAQ What songs do I use in dungeons?
All of them. As always, try to have a song up at all times. Mage's Ballad is going to be the strongest song in AoE, but you have bosses to fight as well. Usually players just stick to the standard song order and buff alignment.
Should I use Stormbite and Caustic Bite on trash pulls?
If you can’t hit three or more targets with Ladonsbite, put Stormbite on things (then Caustic if needed) until you can.
How does Barrage work with Shadowbite?
It increases the potency by 100, and consumes the Barrage buff. It's a gain over Barrage'ing RA on 4 or more targets.

Only you can decide. If you have the upgrade item, the weapon is the most tome-efficient boost you can get. But, if doing Savage your plan is to replace it eventually, so you're delaying your BiS completion by a week. If you think you need the damage output in the short term, or just don't mind the delay, then go for it. If you can't upgrade it, you can do just as well with an upgraded crafted bow, or even better with the 6.3 EX trial bow.

Wearing current gear and letting it sync down will be optimal or close enough not to matter. The most important stat in those fights is HP, and syncing down will max that out. The dps checks are far easier than they were on release, and they were largely easy even then. The one piece that's a non-trivial upgrade is the Shadowbringers i535 relic; if you set that to the ultimate's substat caps (127 - 136 - 184 for UCoB - UWU - TEA respectively) you can get more substats after sync than would otherwise be possible. This is a ~1.5-2% gain.
What food do we use?
Carrot Pudding is our new food for 6.2.
Does Bard have a target Skill Speed?
It isn't crucial, but Being at 2.48 or 2.47 makes it feasible to keep Empyreal Arrow on cooldown without it running into your GCD, while also minimizing song cycle drift.
Why are there melds that don't follow the stat priority?
If you look at the Stats and Tiers table on Etro, you'll see that in FFXIV stats do not provide consistent benefits. Instead, every 13-14 points of Det adds +0.1% to your Det multiplier, every 9-10 points adds to Crit, etc. Because materia gives +36 or +12, it doesn't neatly line up with this. So i.e. a Det X materia could give you +0.2% or +0.3%. The former is far less efficient, to where melding DH X can be slightly better.
Is piece x better than piece y?
The higher item level piece is better. If they're the same ilvl, the one with better substats (see Stats & Melds) is better. A piece with more total substats (i.e. five meld slots) can be better than one the next ilvl step up, but usually they're roughly equal at best and you lose HP for it.

If you're pursuing BiS, follow the BiS set and melds. Otherwise, use these priorities for gear and melds: ITEM LEVEL ( = Weapon Damage and Dexterity) is top priority!! Even over pentamelds. Getting more major stats is better. Substats
1. Critical Hit 2. Determination 3. Direct Hit 4. Skill Speed
The top three are all VERY close, with skill speed a bit further behind. If you have very low crit before melds (< ~1200), it's not worth melding. Make sure to stay under the substat cap when melding! If the number on the meld menu is red (by more than 1-2 points) don't do it. Stats FAQ Is piece x better than piece y?

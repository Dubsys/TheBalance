Slightly more serious answer; go read the guide, each opener leads into different potential rotations and it depends on the fight. What's the point of Celestial Revolution? A consolation prize for when you mess up your PB window. It's lower potency than the other 2 blitzes and single-target, meaning you never want to intentionally use this skill. “Can I gain multiple Chakra from 1 weaponskill?” Yes and no. Say for example you use Rockbreaker on 3 enemies, and it crits every enemy - you will only gain 1 Chakra, as you can only gain 1 chakra per weaponskill. However, if you have Brotherhood on and land a critical weaponskill, you’ll gain 2 chakra; 1 from the crit, 1 from the effect of Brotherhood. Which is better for generating a Lunar Nadi in AoE, Rockbreaker or Shadow of the Destroyer? Shadow of the Destroyer, guaranteed crit > 20 potency (edited)

Is there a way to separate my buffs so I can keep track of Leaden Fist easier? Yes there is; Go into your HUD Layout -> Click your buff bar -> Click the little gearbox button -> Select either Split Element into 3/4 groups. Leaden Fist, Formless Fist, and Perfect Balance all appear on the Conditional Enhancements bar. Everything else is on the Enhancements bar. Why no 1.94 BIS if I play at 60 FPS? The short explanation is that your frame rate and your in-game tooltip interact with each other in a cursed way, so the GCD the game says you have may not necessarily be your actual GCD. By using mr Aya Liz's GCD estimator tool (https://gcdcalc.fly.dev/) you can input a log and see what your GCD actually is. If you score <1.943 while playing with an in-game tooltip of 1.94, continue to play the 1.94 set. If not, it's time to either shift to 1.93 or employ third party tools. The reason for this is that the further above 1.94 you are, the greater the amount of drift your Riddle of Fire will experience. Criterion BiS? What? The reason our normal BiS sets are 1.94/1.93 is to ensure that Riddle of Fire experiences minimal drift over prolonged periods of uptime. Due to the nature of how dungeons are set out this isn't really a problem, and so we can afford to drop some Sks for other substats.

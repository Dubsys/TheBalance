Astrologian Best in Slot (BiS) for 6.2 Go to https://bit.ly/astdmgcalc and then tab to the Patch 6.2(Prelim) tab. We have 2 Tome ring sets and 2 Crafted ring sets. With the advent of Crit finally outscaling Spell Speed… We still are in a position to stack a LOT of Spell Speed. Even when we optimize to minimize Piety and maximize Crit, we have lots of leftover melding slots, and we pick between Spell Speed, and Direct Hit. In terms of damage, the choice between these two is blatantly clear, as Spell Speed is more multiplicative while Direct Hit is more linear. Crafted ring sets provide more damage and should be relatively stable for mana at an endgame relatively-optimized level. The Tome ring sets provide extra safety net (and will be what we use for prog if we get an Ultimate encounter in 6.3). These sets are identical to many other healer sets this tier, thankfully. For cross-compatibility, you can always check other healer resources. I am going to just post the 2.31 sets for the sake of space. If you prefer another GCD, check the spreadsheet. As always, check food. * - 6.3 - What about Augmenting the Rinascita Ring? Due to the loss of advanced melding, there is not a good answer to this. It is so situational, it is quite literally race-dependent. For AST specifically, I do not recommend augmenting your crafted ring if you have access to a pentamelded one. If you do not wish to use/have access to the resources to get a pentamelded crafted ring, go ahead and get an Augmented one.

Melding Astrologian For general purposes:
Crit > DH/Det > SpS.
...however, SpS makes life a bit different for us. Its is a very good stat. Overall better than det and dh point-for-point Be very careful about blindly melding Spell Speed. Unfortunately, this is one of the most dangerous stats to have prioritized, as it has the largest tiers. You can easily go upwards of 50+ almost entirely wasted stats (Almost two whole X materia!) that do quite literally, basically nothing. I would always reference either Akhmorning's Spell Speed stat tiers or etro.gg's built-in tier identification when melding. (https://www.akhmorning.com/allagan-studies/stats/speed/) If you do not care enough to do this, you can always sorta just, meld Crit primarily as safety this tier, fill out with SpS where you can. This will result in a much less likely hunk of wasted stats. ------------------------------------- (edited)

Macros Let me disclaimer and say right away with clarity... MACRO ABILITIES ARE NEVER OPTIMAL FOR AST, EVER. (except for /ac gtoff on Star, that one's okay) No matter what. This includes your <mo>s and <t>s and <me>s. If they help you, that's great, but do not delude yourself into thinking it is optimal. There is one set of macro types I will excuse under the condition that using them, the player understands: 1) It is objectively unoptimal and will cost them DPS and performance 2) They are training wheels that should be worked out of ideally if you wish to improve 3) They will likely cause hiccups with queueing at times and discombobulate you, creating issues where actions are not casted on time or even at all.
KB+M
You can set up a hotbar next to the party list with a bunch of macros that let you Play a card on a target. This is actually a decent visual learning tool. A good guide on how to set it up by Bok https://i.redd.it/p4x9451kqa931.png
Controller
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1obFL3pGEF0w7wHU75o34CWY5N9V5kDTKvl59XMDFTe4/edit This quick document by Aly/Erdene helps illustrate a creative way of getting some macros for controller players by quickly changing your hotbar, letting you press your button, and then changing the hotbar back. I believe it works pretty okay. Her discord if you wish to ask her questions directly is currently Erdene#8008. ---- My personal recommendation is still to simply learn how to manage your actions without needing macros. The techniques are different from KB+M (pre-placing mouse) to controller (pre-setting camera, D-Pad usage). ------------------------------------- (edited)
